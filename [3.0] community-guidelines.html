TitanicMessage - Community Guidelines

Welcome to TitanicMessage!

We’re excited to have you join our community. To make sure everyone enjoys their time here, we’ve outlined a set of guidelines that we kindly ask you to follow. Our goal is to foster a safe, respectful, and fun environment for all users. If you're familiar with Scratch's community, you'll notice some similarities in how we like to operate!

Be Respectful and Kind

TitanicMessage is a place where people come together to share ideas, chat, and have fun. Always be kind, and treat others with respect. Harassment, threats, and hate speech will not be tolerated. This includes:

  • Avoiding offensive language, slurs, or bullying
  • Not sharing explicit content
  • Respecting different opinions, cultures, and perspectives

Keep it Safe for Everyone

To protect our users, we ensure TitanicMessage is a secure space for all. Avoid sharing personal information like your full name, home address, or other sensitive details. We also do not tolerate:

  • Sharing passwords or attempting to hack others’ accounts
  • Promoting scams, spam, or malicious links

Stay On Topic

We encourage fun conversations, but please make sure your messages stay on topic. Irrelevant or spammy content disrupts the chat for others. Please avoid:

  • Repeatedly posting the same messages (spamming)
  • Flooding chats with unnecessary links or ads
  • Derailing conversations from the original topic

Follow Scratch-Inspired Guidelines

Just like Scratch, we value creativity and a collaborative spirit. Here’s how you can contribute positively:

  • Share constructive feedback and encourage others
  • Avoid plagiarism by respecting others’ content
  • Report anything that violates our guidelines instead of engaging with it

Violations and Reporting

If someone breaks the rules, we may remove their content, warn them, or suspend their account. You can help keep TitanicMessage safe by reporting inappropriate behavior or messages through the chat’s report feature.

Thank You for Being Awesome!

By following these guidelines, you help us create a positive, inclusive community. We’re glad to have you here!
